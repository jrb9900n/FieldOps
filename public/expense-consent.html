J.R. Boehlke LLC — Company Card SMS Program

Effective June 12, 2026

Program Description

J.R. Boehlke LLC operates an automated SMS notification program for employees who are issued company credit cards. Messages are sent from +1 (262) 240-6607 for:

  • Transaction alerts when a charge posts to their card
  • Receipt submission reminders for incomplete expense reports
  • Confirmation when a receipt has been received

Message frequency varies based on card usage (typically 1–5 messages per transaction). Message and data rates may apply.

How to Enroll (Opt-In)

Employees enroll by completing the web form at fieldops.jrboehlke.com/enroll.html. The form requires:

  • Your name and mobile phone number
  • Checking a consent checkbox (unchecked by default) confirming you agree to receive automated SMS messages from J.R. Boehlke LLC for company card transaction alerts and expense reminders

After submitting the form, you will receive a confirmation text message. You must reply YES to that message to complete enrollment. This double opt-in step verifies that the phone number belongs to you.

The exact consent language shown on the enrollment form is: "I agree to receive automated SMS text messages from J.R. Boehlke LLC at the number above for company card transaction alerts and expense reminders. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out, HELP for help."

Terms and Conditions

  • Program name: J.R. Boehlke LLC Company Card SMS Notifications
  • Who is eligible: Employees of J.R. Boehlke LLC who have been issued a company credit card.
  • How to enroll: Complete the web form at fieldops.jrboehlke.com/enroll.html and reply YES to the confirmation text (double opt-in).
  • Message frequency: Varies based on card usage. Typically 1–5 messages per transaction (alert, reminders, confirmation).
  • Message and data rates: Standard message and data rates from your mobile carrier may apply.
  • How to opt out: Reply STOP to any message at any time. You will receive one final confirmation and no further messages will be sent. Reply START to re-enroll.
  • How to get help: Reply HELP to any message, or contact us at (262) 242-9924 or michael@jrboehlke.com.
  • Data sharing: Your mobile number will not be shared with or sold to third parties for marketing purposes.
  • Supported carriers: All major US carriers. Carrier is not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

J.R. Boehlke LLC — Privacy Policy

Effective June 2026

Information We Collect

J.R. Boehlke LLC collects the following information from employees who are issued company credit cards:

  • Name and mobile phone number (provided during employment onboarding)
  • Credit card transaction data (received from card issuer alerts)
  • Expense receipts and supporting documentation submitted through this portal

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected solely for internal business purposes:

  • Sending SMS notifications when a charge posts to your company card
  • Sending reminders to submit receipts for expense reporting
  • Confirming receipt of submitted expense documentation
  • Attaching receipts to the corresponding transaction in our accounting system

SMS Messaging

By accepting a company credit card, employees consent to receive automated SMS text messages from +1 (262) 240-6607 for card transaction alerts and expense reminders. Message frequency varies based on card usage. Standard message and data rates may apply.

To opt out of SMS messages, reply STOP to any message. You will receive one confirmation and no further messages will be sent. Reply START to resubscribe. Reply HELP for assistance.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information or mobile phone number with third parties for marketing purposes. Information may be shared only as required by law or with service providers necessary to operate this system (e.g., SMS delivery, accounting software). These providers are bound by confidentiality obligations.

Data Retention

Expense records and receipts are retained for a minimum of seven years in accordance with standard business record-keeping requirements. Mobile phone numbers are removed upon termination of employment.

Full terms and conditions for our SMS program are available at fieldops.jrboehlke.com/expense-consent.html.
